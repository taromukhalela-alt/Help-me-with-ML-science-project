# CAPS Physics Quick Notes

## Mechanics
- Newton's Second Law: F = ma
- Momentum: p = mv
- Kinematics: v = u + at, s = ut + 1/2at^2, v^2 = u^2 + 2as

## Waves and Sound
- Wave speed: v = f * lambda
- Frequency is measured in Hz.

## Electricity and Magnetism
- Ohm's Law: V = IR
- Electrical power: P = VI

## Energy and Change
- Kinetic energy: Ek = 1/2mv^2
- Potential energy near Earth: Ep = mgh
